## 3. How to use this file

- When building decks or briefs, pull from section 2 first ; treat section 1's 🔴 items as examples of what *not* to cite.
- Add new finds under the right reliability tier with date and a one-line "why it matters".
- Pair the risk literature with the learning-science concepts (cognitive load, retrieval, metacognition) so claims about "AI harms learning" are grounded in mechanism, not just opinion.
